Hi, I'm **Toby** 👋 — a language learner, dog lover, and product builder.

I'm building **[Reelang](https://reelang.com)**: a feed of the TikToks, Reels, and Shorts that native speakers actually watch — in the language you're learning. As you scroll, it learns your level and picks the slow, easy ones for you. Videos you can *almost* understand today, and will *fully* understand in a few weeks.

It's **built on comprehensible input** — Reelang starts with speech you can follow, then builds toward how native speakers really sound, across dozens of languages.

I also make my own comprehensible input videos for German learners as **[ThatGermanWord](https://thatgermanword.com)** 🥨 — on TikTok, YouTube, and Instagram.
